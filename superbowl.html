Characterisation of interface astroglial scarring in the human brain after blast exposure: a post-mortem case series

The brains of individuals who experienced one of several types of traumatic brain injury in the context of military service were analysed to determine the nature of the damage done to the brain’s structure by the TBI incident(s).

Hyperbaric oxygen treatment of mild traumatic brain injury persistent post-concussion syndrome and post-traumatic stress disorder

The use of oxygen-rich air is examined as a treatment for mild traumatic brain injury in the case of veteran’s with PTS(D) and a history of TBI.

Concussion, microvascular injury, and early tauopathy in young athletes after impact head injury and an impact concussion mouse model.

Post-mortem examination of teen athletes that experienced Traumatic brain injusry, followed by the development and validation of a mouse model for TBI which is subsequently validated through the use of biochemical, non-invasive imaging, and post mortem electrophysiology.
