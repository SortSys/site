About Us

Science Output Reproducibility Token System (SORTsys) is a global, decentralized marketplace that pays scientists to reproduce and fact-check other scientists’ work.

we are building the Science Output Reproducibility Token System (SORTsys) as a means to pay scientists to fact-check each other. At its core, SORTsys is exchanging one commodity for another: tokens for data. Multiple research groups (unbiased, non-collaborating) will upload their data in order to get paid out from what we call “Science Bounties.” With many sets of reproducibility data stored in an open, immutable, distributed ledger, we can save BILLIONS of wasted research dollars, as well as increase speed and trust in scientific collaboration.

Who Benefits?

Our goal is to improve the scientific ecosystem for all parties involved. We believe that reproducibility is crtiticl for maintaining trust in the community.

Scientists

With SORT, you'll be able to generate additional income by confirming or refuting others' already-published and peer-reviewed science. If you’re good, you may never have to fill out another grant application! Unless, of course, you actually have an idea that will change the course of science. The rest of us will just make sure you are correct.

Grant Makers

The primary source of basic science research grant funding is institutional. This includes governments, grant-making foundations, and private companies’ R&D budget. Institutions will purchase SORTs over the counter and from the market in order to create Science Bounties on isolated, already-published journal articles.

Investors

Our go-to-market strategy is intentionally and perfectly aligned with an initial coin offering (ICO) strategy. We are restricting participation in our ICO to only these types of institutions and their employees. Venture capital and cryptocurrency investors may inquire about investment before the ICO begins.
